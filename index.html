About Us

Tech Syndicate is the prestigious tech club of Amity International School, Sector 46, Gurgaon.

Since its inception in 2016, the Syndicate is constantly working to make technology accessible to everyone and spark an interest in the youth to keep up with the latest developments in the field of computer science.

Our club's motive is to use technology creatively to solve real world problems. Robotronics, our tech symposium, was first hosted in 2013. This contest has truly been a challenge for all the tech wizards from Delhi NCR.

As a legacy we'd like to continue, we're hosting inTech'19 -- an intra-school event simulating the actual Robotronics for providing the students a platform to showcase their talent and creativity in the field of information technology.
