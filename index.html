About Us

Tech Syndicate is the prestigious tech club of Amity International School, Sector 46, Gurgaon.

With its inception in 2016, the club is constantly working to make technology accessible to everyone and spark an interest in the youth to keep up with the latest developments in the field of computer science.

Our club's motive is to use technology creatively to solve real world problems. Robotronics, our tech symposium, was first hosted in 2013. This contest has truly been a challenge for all the tech wizards from Delhi NCR.

As a legacy we'd like to continue, we're hosting inTech '20: an intra-school event simulating the actual Robotronics for providing the students a platform to showcase their talent and creativity in the field of information technology.

prod.io (Audio/Visual Editing)

The year is 2020 and coronavirus has taken over the world. Millions of people are suffering from it and it has caused many countries ordering their citizens to stay at home. Nobody has succeeded in building a vaccine for it. You work at Screenslay Medications and your organisation has found out a solution to this problem. Screenslay has successfully made a medicine for coronavirus but as Screenslay is a very small organization, they can't seem to market it and provide it to the masses. Your job is to make a promotional video for the vaccine of around a minute advertising the vaccine and its cheap price. You're free to make up any features of the medicine or to promote another product with it/promote something else other than the medicine which cures COVID-19. Be sure to make the video so as to flaunt your A/V skills. You can refer to the learning resources to know more about A/V.
Judging Criteria:

  • Originality
  • Smoothness
  • Aesthetic
  • Creativity

Ideatech (Idea Pitching)

Idea Pitching description

3D Design

Coronavirus has changed our lives in many ways in the past few weeks from attending the school on laptops to helping your parents with domestic chores. Some of the household items have become essential be it your digital gadgets, protective masks or even brooms.
Aim: Model a household item which has become an essential item for you.
Submit the .obj file , main scene file , 3-5 rendered images of the model along with a one-line .txt file explaining why is this item an essential for you.
Judging Criteria:

  • Modelling
  • Texturing
  • Lighting
  • Overall appeal

Graphic Design

The goal of designing software is to allow the user to easily explore the interface. In this increasing era of modernity, Music Applications are constantly evolving and users are becoming increasingly savvy to the way interfaces function.
The task for you is to design the User Interface(UI) of a Music Player (either a mobile app or a web player.) Your goal is to make it extremely minimal and user-friendly. You can take the design inspiration from various applications like Spotify, Shazam, Wynk, etc. but your aim should be to create a far more aesthetic and easy-to-use design than those apps.
You will be given bonus points for designing a creative feature of your own.
You will have to share with us your Adobe XD/Adobe Illustrator/Sketch/Adobe Photoshop files along with the Image of each screen of your mobile-app/website.
Check design-resources provided by the Tech Syndicate, if you need any help.
Judgement criterias:

  • User flow
  • Colour consistency
  • Minimalism
  • Creativity
